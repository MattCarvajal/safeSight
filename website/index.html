About this project:

Taking Driver Safety to Another Level

Every single day we all exerpeince many inconviences that affect our goals throughout the day. Whether that goal pertains to going to school, clocking in for work or even picking up food for a date night, all of us are connected by means of transportation. The transportation system we currently use has been affected greatly by one of mankind's latest inventions: the smartphone. The smartphone, like any other device is meant to help us as humans have immediate access to the internet and resources that a conventional desk envoirnment offers, at the luxury of our fingertips. However, one important thing that smartphones and commuting have brought attention to is the distractions that we as humans are sujected to in such a dangerous enviornment. This led to the invention of safesight! Safesight is a system for which is utilized to bring the attention of the driver back to the roads in an effort to effectively bring commute times down, lower accidental occurances on the road, and return driver safety and integrity back to a time when distractions were minimal using current modern technologies. Throughout this website you will find many different documents entialing deep scientific and mathematical analysis of said technoligies, how they opperate, and most importantly the mission Safesight is trying to accomplish.
